== Description ==

Use this plugin to show your Pinterest on your Wordpress site and Facebook tab.

**Pinterest Tab**
1. Engage users to spread your brand on Pinterest.
2. Add it to your fan page.
3. Works on mobile.
4. Boards & Pins
5. Users stay in your environment
6. Easy Setup.

We also offer 14 days free trial of [Pinterest Contest](http://corp.wishpond.com/pinterest-contest/ "Wishpond Pinterest Contest"). 

== Installation ==

1. Download and unzip the Pinterest Tab plugin.
1. Upload `pinterest-tabs` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. You will be automatically redirected to the newly created 'Pinterest tabs' menu within WordPress.
1. From here click 'Create' and you will be able to choose to create your Pinterest tab.
